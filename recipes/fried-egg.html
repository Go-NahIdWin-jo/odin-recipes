Fried Egg

Description

A fried egg is a cooked dish made from one or more eggs which are removed from their shells and placed into a frying pan and fried. They are traditionally eaten for breakfast in many countries but may also be served at other times of the day.(Wikipedia)

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt(optional)
  • Pepper(optional)

Steps

  1. Pour oil on frying pan
  2. Turn on the fire and wait for the oil to be little steamy
  3. Drop the egg
  4. Wait for the bottom of the egg to be brown to get crispy and salty result
  5. Turn off the fire
  6. Serve the fried egg
